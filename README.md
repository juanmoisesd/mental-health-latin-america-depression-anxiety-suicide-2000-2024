## Overview

Comprehensive English-language database on mental health epidemiology across **18 Latin American countries** (2000–2024).

**Author:** Juan Moisés de la Serna Tuya  
**ORCID:** [0000-0002-8401-8018](https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8401-8018)  
**OSF Repository:** [https://osf.io/hwna6/](https://osf.io/hwna6/)

## Contents

The dataset includes country-level data on:

1. **Depression and anxiety** 12-month prevalence rates (World Mental Health Survey, WMHS)
2. **Suicide mortality rates** (age-standardized per 100,000 — WHO/PAHO)
3. **Psychiatrist density** per 100,000 inhabitants (WHO Mental Health Atlas)
4. **Mental health expenditure** as % of total health spending (PAHO)
5. **Treatment gap** estimates (%)
6. **Longitudinal suicide trend** data 2000–2021

## Countries Covered (18)

Argentina, Brazil, Chile, Colombia, Mexico, Peru, Venezuela, Ecuador, Bolivia, Uruguay, Paraguay, Costa Rica, Guatemala, Honduras, El Salvador, Nicaragua, Dominican Republic, Panama
